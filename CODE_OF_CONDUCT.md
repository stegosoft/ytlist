# Code of Conduct

## Our Standard

Please keep discussions respectful, technical, and focused on improving the
project.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment or personal attacks
- Spam or intentionally disruptive behavior
- Publishing private information without permission

## Enforcement

Project maintainers may moderate discussions, issues, and pull requests to keep
the project usable and constructive.

## Contact

For conduct concerns, contact `stegosoft@gmail.com`.
